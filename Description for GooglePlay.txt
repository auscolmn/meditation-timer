  Full Description

  Find your calm with Sati — a beautifully simple meditation timer designed to help you build a lasting practice.

  "Sati" is the Pali word for mindfulness and awareness, the foundation of meditation.

  SIMPLE YET POWERFUL
  Set your meditation duration, choose calming sounds, and begin. No complicated features, no distractions — just you and your practice.

  TRACK YOUR PROGRESS
  • Daily streaks keep you motivated
  • Visual calendar shows your meditation history
  • Unlock achievements from 3 days to 1 year
  • See total sessions and time invested in yourself

  CUSTOMIZABLE SOUNDS
  • Tibetan singing bowl, gong, and chime bells
  • Relaxing rain and waterfall backgrounds
  • Set interval bells to stay grounded
  • Upload your own custom sounds

  DAILY INSPIRATION
  • Curated quotes from Buddhist, Taoist, and Stoic wisdom
  • New quote every day to reflect upon
  • Add your own favorite quotes

  DESIGNED FOR FOCUS
  • Clean, distraction-free interface
  • Works completely offline
  • Keeps your screen awake during sessions
  • No ads, no accounts, no tracking

  YOUR DATA STAYS YOURS
  All your meditation data is stored locally on your device. No cloud, no servers, no accounts required.

  One-time purchase. No subscriptions. Ever.

  Whether you're new to meditation or have an established practice, Sati provides the simple tools you need to show up for yourself, day
  after day.

  Begin your journey inward.
